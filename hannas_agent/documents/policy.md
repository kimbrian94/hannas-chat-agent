# Cancellation & Refund Policy

### Are deposits refundable?

Deposits are non-refundable by default.

**Exceptions (documentation required):**
- Medical emergencies (e.g., miscarriage or premature birth) → 100% refund
- Overseas relocation with proof (e.g., flight ticket) → 100% refund

### Cancellation before service begins

Refund amounts are based on how early the cancellation is made:

- **120+ days before start date** → 90% refund
- **Within 120 days** → 75% refund
- **Within 90 days** → 50% refund
- **Within 60 days** → 25% refund
- **Within 30 days** → No refund

### Cancellation after service has started

Refunds are calculated after deducting services already provided, plus the following penalties:

**Short-term cancellation (1–4 service days used)**
→ 1 additional service day is non-refundable

**Long-term cancellation (5+ service days used)**
→ 1 full week of service is non-refundable

### Refund Processing

All approved refunds are processed within 4 business weeks.

Refund calculations will deduct:
- Any applicable cancellation penalties
- Services already completed
- Any outstanding unpaid balances
