# POSTPARTUM DOULA SERVICES

## What is a Postpartum Doula?

A postpartum doula is a trained professional who provides physical, emotional, and informational support to families during the postpartum period. Unlike medical professionals, doulas focus on the family's comfort, education, and adjustment to life with a newborn.

## Our Doula Services Include

### Newborn Care Support:
- Safe sleep practices and SIDS prevention
- Diapering and bathing techniques
- Umbilical cord and circumcision care
- Recognizing hunger and sleep cues
- Soothing techniques for fussy babies
- Developmental milestones guidance
- Baby wearing instruction

### Infant Feeding Support:
- Breastfeeding positioning and latch assistance
- Bottle feeding guidance
- Pumping schedules and storage
- Formula preparation
- Introduction of solid foods (for extended care)
- Managing feeding challenges

### Mother's Recovery:
- Physical recovery monitoring (C-section or vaginal delivery)
- Rest and sleep optimization
- Postpartum exercise guidance
- Emotional support and reassurance
- Postpartum depression/anxiety awareness
- Self-care encouragement

### Household Support:
- Light housekeeping (dishes, laundry, tidying)
- Meal preparation and planning
- Grocery shopping assistance
- Errand running
- Sibling care and activities
- Pet care coordination

### Family Adjustment:
- Partner/spouse support and education
- Sibling adjustment assistance
- Grandparent and visitor management
- Establishing family routines
- Work-life balance planning

## Service Options

### DAYTIME SUPPORT (8am - 5pm)
- 4-hour minimum shifts
- Typical schedule: 9am-1pm or 1pm-5pm
- Focus on education, household support, and baby care
- Mother gets rest time while doula cares for baby
- **Rate: $45/hour**

### EVENING SUPPORT (5pm - 10pm)
- Assistance during "witching hour"
- Dinner preparation and cleanup
- Bedtime routine establishment
- Evening household tasks
- Partner education and involvement
- **Rate: $45/hour**

### OVERNIGHT CARE (10pm - 8am)
- Mother sleeps while doula cares for baby
- All diaper changes and soothing
- Brings baby to mother for breastfeeding (if applicable)
- Bottle feeding for formula or pumped milk
- Detailed morning report of night activities
- **Rate: $55/hour (10-hour minimum)**

### EXTENDED SHIFTS (12+ hours)
- Full day or full night coverage
- Ideal for single parents or partners returning to work
- Comprehensive care allowing extended rest periods
- **Rate: $50/hour for 12+ hour shifts**

## What to Expect

### First Visit:
- Meet your doula and establish rapport
- Review birth experience and current challenges
- Assess baby's feeding, sleeping, and health
- Create personalized care plan
- Set goals and priorities
- Tour home and locate supplies
- Duration: Usually 4-5 hours

### Ongoing Visits:
- Consistent care from same doula when possible
- Flexibility to adjust focus areas as needs change
- Written daily care logs
- Text/email support between visits
- Coordination with pediatrician when needed

## Scheduling

- Book during pregnancy or after birth
- Flexible start date
- Shifts scheduled around your needs
- Last-minute scheduling based on availability
- Can extend hours or add services anytime

## Doula Matching Process

We carefully match families with doulas based on:
- Location and schedule availability
- Personality and communication style
- Language preferences (English, Korean)
- Special circumstances (multiples, special needs)
- Cultural and religious considerations
- Specific skills or experience requested

*Meet-and-greet option available before commitment.*
