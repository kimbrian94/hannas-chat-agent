# POSTPARTUM MEAL DELIVERY SERVICE

## Why Nutrition Matters Postpartum

Proper nutrition is crucial for:
- Physical recovery from childbirth
- Milk production for breastfeeding mothers
- Energy levels and mood stability
- Wound healing (C-section or perineal tears)
- Hormone regulation
- Immune system support
- Mental clarity and emotional wellbeing

## Our Approach

We specialize in postpartum-specific nutrition combining:
- Traditional Korean Sanhujori principles
- Modern nutritional science
- Lactation-supporting ingredients
- Anti-inflammatory foods
- Easy-to-digest options
- Iron and protein-rich meals

### All meals are:
- Chef-prepared with fresh, high-quality ingredients
