# HANNA'S MOM'S CARE - COMPANY OVERVIEW

## About Us

Hanna's Mom's Care is a premier postpartum care service dedicated to supporting new mothers and families during the critical first weeks and months after childbirth. Founded in 2020 in Seattle, Washington, we provide professional, compassionate care that helps families navigate the joys and challenges of welcoming a new baby.

Our mission is to ensure every mother receives the rest, support, and guidance she needs to recover fully and bond with her newborn. We believe that proper postpartum care is essential for the health and wellbeing of both mother and baby.

## Our Values

- **Compassionate Care:** We treat every family with kindness, respect, and understanding
- **Professional Excellence:** Our team maintains the highest standards of training and certification
- **Cultural Sensitivity:** We honor diverse postpartum traditions, especially Korean Sanhujori practices
- **Holistic Support:** We address physical, emotional, and practical needs of new families
- **Empowerment:** We educate parents to build confidence in their new roles
